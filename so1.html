Funding Campaign of Mangwon-dong
Seoul, South Korea

‘모이자 망원’은 망원동 거주민과 방문객들 사이의 갈등을 감소할 수 있는 펀딩 캠페인으로, 망원동의 ‘모이는’, ‘쌓인’ 등의 키워드를 통한 패턴 아이덴티티를 가진다. 이 패턴은 매체 또는 환경에 따라 패턴의 다양한 변주가 가능한 브랜딩 아이덴티티 (Interacive Branding Identity)로 사용되었다.

This project is a funding campaign for reducing conflict between residents and visitors of the town which suffers from ‘gentrification’.
By the web interactive card, the visitors visit a website of the campaign, experiencing <Making my Manwon-dong> and <Appreciate its history by collected images> pages. On the other way, by participating funding campaign to help the issue of the town, this campaign can become an invitation for residents from visitors. The brand identity is inspired by the keyword, “gather” which finds easily in the town. Moreover, its elements of graphics have a common characteristic called “Communal” and they are visualized from “Communal Parking Lot”, “Local Authority Homes” and so on respectively. Additionally, these graphics are applied to diverse sections using a variation of patterns.
